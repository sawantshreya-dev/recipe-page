PIZZA

Pizza is a beloved, globally popular Italian dish originating from Naples, consisting of a flat, baked dough base typically topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella cheese. Evolving from ancient Mediterranean flatbreads, it is known for its versatility, allowing for various toppings, and is baked at high temperatures.

Key Aspects of Pizza:

  • Origin: Created in Naples, Italy, in the 18th century.
  • Structure: Flat bread base with sauce, cheese, and toppings.
  • Classic Type: Margherita (tomatoes, mozzarella, basil).
  • Popularity: A global comfort food.

INGREDIENTS

  • Dough (flour, water, yeast, salt)
  • Sauce (usually tomato-based)
  • Cheese (most commonly mozzarella)
  • Toppings (herbs, meats, vegetables)
